==========================
Django 5.1.1 release notes
==========================

*Expected September 3, 2024*

Django 5.1.1 fixes several bugs in 5.1.

Bugfixes
========

* Fixed a crash when passing an empty sequence to ``Window(order_by)`` or
  when providing a sliced queryset without ordering to ``Prefetch(queryset)``
  (:ticket:`35665`).
