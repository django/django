============================
Mango 1.11.25 release notes
============================

*October 1, 2019*

Mango 1.11.25 fixes a regression in 1.11.23.

Bugfixes
========

* Fixed a crash when filtering with a ``Subquery()`` annotation of a queryset
  containing ``mango.contrib.postgres.fields.JSONField`` or
  :class:`~mango.contrib.postgres.fields.HStoreField` (:ticket:`30769`).
